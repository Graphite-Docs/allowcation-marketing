Allowcation

Allowance + Allocation = Allowcation

A simple application for tracking your child's allowance and financial allocations. All without having to deal in cash.

Enter debits and credits and always know how much money your child has to spend from their allownace (or other allocations). As an added bonus, see what your child's balance would be if they had invested in Bitcoin.
